Homey Pages
Personal Websites from Berlin
We offer websites that make it easy for you to present your ideas, projects or youself.

We enjoy it when people show what they can offer to others, and we believe that everyone can handle his/her own website, learn how to change it … with a little help.

Our websites fit for people who want to understand how it is built, and grow the courage to change it themselves, or to trust us – pass your photos and texts over to us and be courious what comes out of it.

Our Offers for You

A website is the entrance from the world wide web to your world.

Basic

  • Artful & responsive website, based on your text and images

    You give us the freedom to use our artistic talents and we try to create something beautiful out of your texts and images. “Responsive” ist a technical term and means that the website look nice of smartphones, tablets and large screens.

  • Choose any free StartBootstrap theme

    The website StartBootstrap.com is a great source for beautiful, modern and functional website templates. They have many free themes. Choose one of them to start with …

  • Reorganisation of sections

    All StartBootstrap themes are organized in sections, like this one, where you find our three offers, or the next, where you can learn about how a website is built. Sections are very flexible. You can swap them, duplicate (in most cases), take them out, whatever you like.

  • Choose up to 5 photos from gallery

    Further below you find a gallery with photos made by Maarja. You can choose up to five of them for free, which is a value of about €150 normally.

  • Edit website yourself

    When your website is ready, you will be able to modify it. We will show you how. Changing text and images is quite simple. Fonts or layout are more complicated, but in theory everything can be changed by you. There are now limits.

  • Free website hosting, with backup

    GitHub is the best place in the world to host (keep, store) your projects, your website in this case. For example, you can go back to whatever version you want, if somethings goes wrong, anytime. And the new function GitHub pages lets you publish a website automatically, when you change something. And its free.

  • optional: use your own web address

    With a little technical effort, you can make GitHub use your own web address, any web address. We will tell you how, when you want to do that.

Personal

  • Based on BASIC or PREMIUM offer

    The following offers we can make, when you already got a website from us, or are in the process of getting one.

  • Personal photo session and filming (make it personal)

    Maarja is professional photographer. Together with her you can have a photo session at places you like. Or spend a day or two together for a short movie about yourself. One example you can find here

  • Hugo Static Site Generator (manage lots of images, text and other media)

    If you want to change your website yourself regularly: New videos, texts, blogs, images, keep it alive, you will love Hugo. Hugo makes it very easy to change things in a breeze.

  • Programming addons/changes to website (whatever you want)

    Michael is professional programmer. And sometimes you get an idea how your website could be even better, cooler or more functional. Then talk to Michael. There are not limits in programming, everything is possible. There are just limits in time.

  • Drawings, art work, get a logo

    Maarja again: You might need some special art work, like drawings, or music, or a logo. Let yourself be surprised, again and again. :-)

  • Technical Coaching (if you have questions …)

    You would really like to learn more about this … machine. But you think it’s too complicated, or you will get stuck every now and then. You will never understand enough of it, that you can get playful creative. Then you have been tricked by a wrong thought. Talk to Michael. ;-)
